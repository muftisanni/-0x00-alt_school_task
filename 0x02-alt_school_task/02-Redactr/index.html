Redactr-App

Redactr-App is an a web app that could help you safely share content on social media by scrambling portions of texts you wouldn't want the world to see/read.

Mode of operation

  1. Write/paste the content into the specified form field
  2. Specify the words you need to be scrambled into the specified input field below the form field. Note: more than one word are separated by space
  3. Specify what to use as replacement for the words that you want to scramble. For dashes (-), underscores (_), example asteriks (*), question marks (?), or even other words.
  4. Click on the "Redactr Now" button to redact your content
